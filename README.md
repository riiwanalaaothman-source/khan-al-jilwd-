##  Project Overview
[Khan El Galoud is a premium Egyptian handmade leather brand established in 2020, specializing 
in personalized genuine leather products. The brand focuses on creating meaningful, customized gifts such as leather wallets, 
card holders, clutches, and accessories made from 100% natural leather with high-quality craftsmanship.

The project aims to solve a common problem in the gifting market: the lack of unique, personal, and emotionally valuable gifts. 
Many available options are generic and lack a personal touch. Khan El Galoud addresses this by offering customized products that can be 
personalized with names, messages, or photos, transforming each item into a memorable and meaningful gift.

The project will be presented through a strong digital presence, including social media platforms, visual content, 
and storytelling-based marketing that highlights the emotional value of gift giving. The focus will be on building a brand that connects with
 customers on a personal level while showcasing the quality and authenticity of handmade leather products..]

---

## Project Objectives
-Build a strong brand identity for Khan El Galoud as a premium handmade leather gift brand

-Boost sales by promoting personalized leather products as meaningful gift options
